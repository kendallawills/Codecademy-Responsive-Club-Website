Summerville Art Club

Where Passion

Meets Purpose

Summerville Art Club was established in 2015 by a dedicated group of artists, educators, and community leaders in response to the need for hands-on art education in Summerville. Located in the heart of Downtown Summerville, it's the area's only nonprofit community art center providing hands-on arts instruction and engagement. Our vision is to be a recognized hub for accessible arts education and experiences through our work to to energize and empower the arts community.

GALLERIES ARE FREE AND OPEN TO THE PUBLIC!

Tuesday - Friday: 9 AM - 5 PM

Saturday: 11 AM - 3 PM

TODDLER TUESDAYS

Every Tuesday from 2 PM - 5 PM bring your children to create works of art with our wonderful teachers. Parents are encouraged to paint as well!

FIRST FRIDAYS

Visit on the first friday of the month from 6 PM - 9 PM to view exhibitions in the Main Gallery and Community Gallery, meet the Studio Artists, buy local art, and participate in special family art activities!

GROUP TOURS

Free Tours for Groups between 8 - 50 Attendees

Book a group tour of our gallery and studios for your school field trip, work outing, or large family activity! The minimum age for children is 6 years old. We are an accessible building with a ramp at the front of the building and an elevator to get to the second floor. Contact us to book your tour!
